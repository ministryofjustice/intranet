Troubleshooting

You are on a MoJ network but still seeing the 401 authorisation required page?

Sometimes your browser cannot tell when you've switched to a secure MoJ network and will continue to display the 401 authorisation required page. This can happen on a phone, tablet or laptop. To fix this, once you are sure you are connected to an MoJ network/wifi or VPN, completely close down the browser and any other browser windows you may have open on your device. Then reopen the browser and try the intranet address again.

For further information about VPN access, contact your department service desk for more info.

If you feel that you have reached this page in error, please email intranet-support@digital.justice.gov.uk.
